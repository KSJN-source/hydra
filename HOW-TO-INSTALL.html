💧 Hydra

Water Tracker — Install as a Web App

Step 1 — Upload to Netlify (free, 2 min)

1

Go to app.netlify.com/drop

2

Drag and drop the hydra-pwa folder onto the page

3

Netlify gives you a free URL like https://random-name.netlify.app
You can rename it to something like hydra-sujan.netlify.app in the site settings

Step 2 — Install on Android

1

Open the Netlify URL in Chrome

2

Tap the ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen (or Chrome may show an install banner automatically)

3

Tap Install — Hydra appears on your home screen like a native app

Step 2 — Install on iPhone

1

Open the Netlify URL in Safari (must be Safari for iOS install)

2

Tap the Share ↑ button at the bottom of the screen

3

Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen

4

Tap Add — Hydra appears on your home screen and opens full screen

What you get

✅  Full screen — no browser chrome
✅  Works offline after first load
✅  Data saved on device (localStorage)
✅  Home screen icon with Hydra drop logo
✅  No app store, no account needed
